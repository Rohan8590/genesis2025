About Genesis

Welcome to Genesis 2025

Genesis is the annual cultural fest of our institution, bringing together creative minds from across the country. With a legacy of excellence, Genesis 2025 promises to be bigger and better than ever before.

This year, we're celebrating the spirit of innovation, creativity, and cultural diversity through a series of exciting events, competitions, and performances.

TechNiti

An infographic event to showcase graphic visual representations to convey ideas. Team FORETech

Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra is a national-level management case competition held during Genesis, where B-school teams solve real-world, cross-functional business problems. The event includes an online quiz, a PPT-based solution submission, and an offline final presentation before academic and corporate experts. It tests participants’ analytical thinking, creativity, and strategic problem-solving.

Team CID

Jagrukta

Jagrukta is a nukkad natak competition that uses street theatre to highlight critical social issues through live performances evaluated by expert judges.

Team ANTAR

10:00 AM

Prerna

PRERNA is a CSR-focused case study competition organized by Team ANTAR in collaboration with Team CSD. It consists of an online CSR quiz, an online case submission, and a final offline presentation and tests participants’ ability to integrate social and environmental responsibility into practical business solutions. Team ANTAR & Team CSD

3:30 PM

Eleganza

Genesis is the institution’s flagship two-day festival, bringing together cultural and corporate events organized by student committees. It attracts participants from top B-schools, offering a platform to showcase talent, skills, and creativity. Team Nexus

10:00 AM

Argumentative Indian

The Argumentative Indian is an inter-college mock parliament simulation proposed by SIG Sankalp and FORE Word, where students role-play legislators to debate and resolve policy issues. The event includes an online quiz followed by an on-campus parliamentary simulation representing Indian states. It develops policy analysis, structured argumentation, negotiation, and collaborative decision-making skills. Team FOREword & Team SIG-Sankalp

10:00 AM

Raffle 8.0

RAFFLE 8.0 is the flagship event of the International Business Forum (IBF), focused on global trade, exports, and imports. The competition includes a world-map-based quiz, a country bidding round, and a final presentation on trade facilitation and innovation. Team IBF

10:00 AM

Stratagem

Stratagem is a PR-focused competition where participants tackle branding and crisis-management challenges. Teams manage a celebrity-related PR crisis, design a damage-control campaign, and propose a new celebrity endorsement aligned with brand values. Team FORE Connect

11:00 AM

People In Chaos

People in the Chaos is an HR-focused management innovation challenge by SIG Unstop and SIG HR that simulates real-world people management dilemmas. The event includes an online management quiz, a constraint-based HR solution submission, and an offline final pitch. Team SIG-HR &SIG-Unstop Igniters

09:30 AM

TechnoSlides

Case study competition focused on IT technologies. Team FORETech

09:30 AM

Step Up: Western and Streets

Step Up is a dance featuring both a main-stage western dance event and a street-style battle format. Teams compete through choreographed performances and live dance battles judged on skill, creativity, and execution. Team CDF

9:30 AM

Concoct

CONCOCT is an inter-institute B-Plan pitching competition which invites teams from top B-schools to present startup ideas or existing ventures before an expert jury of entrepreneurs and alumni. The event promotes innovation, entrepreneurship, and practical business pitching skills through a structured on-campus competition. Team IIC

10:00 AM

Guilty As Charged

Guilty as Charged is a flagship legal mock trial competition centered on defending controversial public figures. The event features an online quiz followed by two intense offline rounds involving argumentation, rebuttals, and courtroom-style trials. Team PEC

10:00 AM

Prompt-Verse

PromptVerse is an AI-focused competition that introduces students to prompt engineering and effective AI utilization. The event includes an online prompt-based treasure hunt, a one-slide solution submission, and an offline final presentation solving a live AI problem. Team SIGnalytics

11:00 AM

Corporate Buddha

A case-based strategy competition which challenges students to solve real-world business problems aligned with the current corporate environment. It includes an online quiz round, a case-solution round, and an on-campus final presentation evaluated by industry experts, focusing on strategic thinking and practical problem-solving skills. Team Think Tank

10:00 AM

Global Frugal Genius

Global Frugal Genius competition focused on frugal innovation and cost-efficient problem-solving. The event includes an online quiz, a short case submission proposing low-cost solutions, and an offline campus presentation for finalists. Team CRIFT

10:00 AM

The PR-edicament

The PR-edicament is a crisis management and PR strategy competition where teams tackle ethical, operational, and reputational crises through quizzes, case-based video pitches, and an offline courtroom-style showdown. Team SIGMA & FOSTRA

11:00 AM

Capital Quest: Think. Build. React

An inter-college finance competition by FEFF and SIG-IP that combines equity research, portfolio creation, and live market simulation. Participants progress through a quiz, portfolio PPT submission, and an offline investment simulation followed by a strategy presentation. Team FEFF & SIG‑IP
